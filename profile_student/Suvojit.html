Currently, in IEM, I am exploring the field of VLSI circuit domain in electronics and communication engineering, participating in an internship at the IEDC ECE labs and previously participated in the training course of Jadavpur University for the same. I merge my passion for VLSI circuit design, data analytics and AI/ML. certain skills that I have worked with include web development(HTML, CSS and JavaScript), machine learning(Python), real-life problem solving by Product building, Analog and digital circuit design. My contributions to innovative projects that promote recycling and sustainability, including the "Plastic Refill Recycler" and "Waste Oil Recycler" have been significant. In addition, I have also been involved with a hardware project called "Magnetic Lock Sensor" which further demonstrates my dedication to innovation and the welfare of society.
